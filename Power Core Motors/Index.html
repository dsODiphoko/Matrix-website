The Infinitum PowerCore is a revolutionary electric motor for
mining applications, delivering the same power as traditional
motors in a package that’s 50% smaller and significantly
lighter. Designed with a patented PCB stator and integrated
VFD controls, PowerCore combines motor, intelligence and
control in a compact, space-saving design that is ideal for
mobile and stationary mining equipment.
