Safety Essentials

As operations move toward automation, a strong safety
foundation is more important than ever. Safety Essentials
acknowledges the critical supporting systems—beyond collision
avoidance and control—that are required to keep people,
equipment, and production secure. These technologies address
hazards that automation alone can’t solve, such as gas
detection, environmental monitoring, and enhanced operator
awareness.

Matrix’s portfolio of safety essentials includes proven solutions
like the M5 machine-mounted methane monitor, advanced camera
systems, and environmental sensors. Together, these systems
provide the real-time data, compliance support, and operational
confidence needed for a safer, smarter, and more resilient path to
automation.
